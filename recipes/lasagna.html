Lasagna 2.0

Ever wanted to make the best lasagna in the world?

With some ground beef, some garlic and fresh tomato sauce, this bad boy will take you to the skies!

What you will need:

  • 2 Yellow onions
  • 2 Cloves of garlic
  • 500g Ground beef
  • 4 Tomato puree
  • 1 Meat bouillon cube

Step by step:

  1. Chop the onions to small bits and fry them.

  2. Fry the garlic with the yellow onions and go to the next step.

  3. Put the ground beef with the garlic and the onions and season with pepper.

  4. Add tomato puree and let it blend for a minute

  5. Put it all in a stove and leave it for about 40 minutes, then serve it!
